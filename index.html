Welcome to this guide!

Here, you can learn everything you need to know about Scroll Velocities (SVs) in Quaver.

The guide is split into 3 main topics:

  1. Mapping - How to map with SVs to create cool effects (note go brrrrrrrrrr)
  2. Playing - How to play SV maps and practice them effectively (resolve skill vissue)
  3. Philosophy - How to think about SVs (they are cool 😎).

You can explore each topic in any order. Hopefully you can get something helpful out of each section.
